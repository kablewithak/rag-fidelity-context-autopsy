Northstar Support Policy — Synthetic Sample

Standard Support
Standard support requests receive an initial response within two business days. Business days exclude weekends and public holidays in the customer's contracted support region.

Enterprise Support
Enterprise customers receive an initial response within four business hours for Severity 1 incidents and within one business day for Severity 2 incidents.

Refund Policy
A customer may request a refund within 14 calendar days of the first paid subscription charge when the service has not been materially used. Refund requests must be submitted through the billing portal.

Security Notification
If Northstar confirms unauthorized access to customer content, Northstar will notify the affected customer without undue delay and no later than seventy-two hours after confirmation.
