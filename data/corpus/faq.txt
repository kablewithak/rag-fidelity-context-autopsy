Northstar Product FAQ — Synthetic Sample

Q: How do I export workspace data?
A: Workspace owners can export workspace data from Settings > Data Management > Export. A download link is sent to the owner's verified email address.

Q: How do I delete a workspace?
A: Only workspace owners may request deletion. Submit the deletion request from Settings > Data Management > Delete Workspace. The request starts a seven-day recovery window.

Q: Can I change my billing plan?
A: Workspace owners can change plans from Settings > Billing. Changes to annual plans take effect at the next renewal unless otherwise stated in an order form.
